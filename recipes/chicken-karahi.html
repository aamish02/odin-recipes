Chicken Karahi


Description

Chicken Karahi is a delicious food in Pakistan which is origin from Sindh and is now most popular food in Pakistan.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Masala
  • Spice

Steps

  1. Put chicken in pot
  2. Add water
  3. Stain boil rice
  4. Add chicken Masala
  5. Heat it
